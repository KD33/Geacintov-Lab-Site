THE GEACINTOV LABORATORY

DNA Damage & Repair

MAIN INTERESTS

The interests of the laboratory are on DNA damage caused by environmental polynuclear aromatic cancer-causing contaminants and endogenous free radical intermediates that are overproduced in humans at sites of inflammation.

FOCUS

The focus of our studies is on mechanisms of formation of DNA lesions, their stereochemical and structural properties, and their repair by the well known multi-tasking cellular defense mechanism called nucleotide excision repair.
